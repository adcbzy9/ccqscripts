Address Change

BEFORE ASKING FOR THE NEW ADDRESS CHECK TO SEE IF THERE IS BANKING INFO ON FILE

“Before I change your address I just want to inform you that when an address is changed it places a 30 day hold on redemptions sent by check to the new address.
Do you want to hear about how to redeem during the hold period?”

If “Yes”:
If the bank is on file:
“The monies can be sent to ____ bank (name), account with the last 4 digits ending in ____.”
If the bank is not on file:
“You can send in a redemption form or letter of instructions with a signature guarantee stamp beside your signature during the next 30 days after the address change.”

Ask the caller for the new address and phone # (if it applies)

Enter the information in CRM

Confirm back to the caller the new address and phone #

Submit the address/phone # change in CRM

Provide the confirmation # to the caller

“A confirmation letter will be sent to your old address, arriving within 7 to 10 business days.”
