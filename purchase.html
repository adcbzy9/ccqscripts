BEFORE PROCESSING THE PURCHASE

1) Confirm the purchase amount, and into which fund - check on PSS Hub and the SB-70 Approved Fund List that purchases can be made into the fund.

2) Confirm to the caller the banking information (Pay Access) on the account
Master Menu > Addresses > Pay Access instructions

3) IRA Accounts (prior to the tax filing deadline) - Ask the caller if the contribution will be for the prior or current year

4) Process the purchase in the CRM - If the purchase amount per fund is over $25,000 create a Trade Log E-Form in Web Image
