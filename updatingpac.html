UPDATING A PAC

1) Confirm back to the caller the existing PAC (date, amount, frequency, bank and last 4 digits of the bank acct #).
“Your current automatic investment plan is scheduled to run monthly on the 12th of the month for $100 from Wells Fargo Bank, with the last 4 digits of the bank account ending in 4445.”

2) Clarify the type of maintenance (permanent stop / stop for certain months / change date, amount, frequency).

3) Make the change(s) in CRM (DO NOT HIT THE SUBMIT BUTTON).

4) Summarize the details of the PAC change(s) and obtain consent from the caller.
Ex: “I am changing the automatic investment plan to run monthly, on the 25th of the month for $200. Is that correct?”

5) SUBMIT the update in CRM.

6) Offer a confirmation number.
“Would you like to write down the confirmation number for this change today?”

7) Mention the confirmation letter.
“A confirmation letter will be sent to your current address arriving within 7 to 10 business days.”

8) Inform the caller about making future changes.
“If you wish to make any changes in the future you must call at least 3 business days before the scheduled date for the changes to be effective for that month.”
