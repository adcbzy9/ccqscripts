Exchange

BEFORE PROCESSING THE EXCHANGE:

1) Confirm the trade details -
  • The amount - if the amount, per fund, is over $25,000 create a Trade Log E-Form in image
  • The TO fund name, class & ticker symbol
  • The FROM fund name, class & ticker symbol
  • The account number/numbers involved - You can exchange within an account and from a fund in a Voluntary account into a fund in an IRA account (Transfer to IRA in CRM)
2) Confirm if TO fund is open to purchases - check on PSS Hub and the SB-70 (Approved Fund List)

3) If a PAC is active on the FROM fund, provide the PAC details and ask if the caller would like it to continue.

  • If No: Master Menu > Inquiry > PAC > Permanent Stop remember to click the box under Stop, then Submit

  • If Yes: nothing needs to be done

  • If Yes, but on the TO fund:
  • Stop the PAC on the from Fund Master Menu > Inquiry > PAC > Permanent Stop
  • Click on Add PAC for the new fund on PAC Detail Screen. Master Menu Inquiry > PAC > Add PAC or Master Menu > Maintain > Add PAC
  • Enter the PAC details



4) Process the exchange in CRM - again, if the amount, per fund, is over $25,000 create a Trade Log E-Form in Web image.

BEFORE SUBMITTING THE EXCHANGE:

"YOU HAVE REQUESTED AN EXCHANGE WITHIN YOUR ACCOUNT ENDING IN XXXX THE AMOUNT OF _______ FROM (fund/class) _______ INTO (fund/Class) _______. THIS EXCHANGE WILL BE PROCESSED AS OF THE MARKET CLOSE _______ (date in CRM). A CONFIRMATION LETTER WILL BE SENT OUT THE NEXT BUSINESS DAY, ARRIVING TO THE ADDRESS OF RECORD WITHIN 7 TO 10 BUSINESS DAYS.



VOLUNTARY ACCOUNT ONLY: IF IT APPLIES, A TAX FORM WILL BE GENERATED AND MAILED TO YOU THE EARLY PART OF NEXT YEAR.

MAY I PLEASE HAVE YOUR TELEPHONE NUMBER? DO I HAVE PERMISSION TO SUBMIT YOUR REQUEST?
WOULD YOU LIKE THE CONFIRMATION NUMBER FOR THE EXCHANGE? THE CONFIRMATION NUMBER IS _______"
