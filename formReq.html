Form Request

1) PROVIDE THE NAME OF THE FORM
“IN ORDER TO ___________ YOU ARE REQUIRED TO FILL OUT _____________."

2) PROVIDE AT LEAST 2 DELIVERY OPTIONS, WITH ONE BEING THE FASTEST, FOR THE FORM TO ARRIVE TO THE CALLER
“I CAN DIRECT YOU TO OUR WEBSITE TO DOWNLOAD THE FORM, OR I CAN EMAIL THE FORM TO YOU.”

3) CONFIRM THE ADDRESS TO SEND THE FORM, AND PROVIDE THE TIME FRAME FOR THE FORM TO ARRIVE TO THE CALLER
“TO CONFIRM YOUR EMAIL ADDRESS IS ________. THE FORM SENT VIA EMAIL WILL ARRIVE WITHIN 20 MINUTES.”

4) OFFER ASSISTANCE WITH FILLING OUT THE FORM ONCE IT ARRIVES
  • IF DOWNLOADED BY THE CALLER OFFER TO EXPLAIN HOW TO FILL OUT EACH SECTION OF THE FORM.
  • IF SENDING THE FORM TO THE CALLER:
    “IF YOU HAVE ANY QUESTIONS FILLING OUT THE FORM ONCE IT ARRIVES, FEEL FREE TO GIVE US A CALL.”
5) PROVIDE AT LEAST 2 DELIVERY OPTIONS FOR THE FORM TO BE SENT IN
“THE MAILING ADDRESS IS ON THE FORM OR YOU CAN FAX IT IN USING FAX # _____”

6) PROVIDE THE PROCESSING DETAILS AND TIMEFRAMES ONCE THE FORM IS SENT IN
  • NON-FINANCIAL:
    “ONCE YOUR FORM IS RECEIVED IN GOOD ORDER, BEFORE 4 PM EASTERN TIME ON A BUSINESS DAY, IT WILL BE PROCESSED WITHIN 3 TO 5 BUSINESS DAYS……………..”
  • FINANCIAL:
    “ONCE YOUR FORM IS RECEIVED IN GOOD ORDER, BEFORE 4 PM EASTERN TIME ON A BUSINESS DAY, THE REDEMPTION WILL BE PROCESSED WITH THAT DAY’S MARKET CLOSING PRICE……………”
7) OFFER A CONFIRMATION # FOR THE FORM REQUEST IF YOU ARE SENDING THE FORM TO THE CALLER
“WOULD YOU LIKE TO WRITE DOWN A CONFIRMATION NUMBER FOR THE FORM REQUEST?”

LOCATE THE FORM USING:
  • PSS HUB or
  • Primerica.com / Mutual Fund Account / Help / Service Forms
  • MAKE SURE TO REVIEW THE INSTRUCTIONS ON THE FIRST PAGE OF THE FORM to determine if any additional form or stamp is required. That information needs to be explained to the caller.

Examples

NONFINANCIAL

  • normally processed within 3 to 5 business days if it arrives in GOOD ORDER before 4pm ET on a business day. A confirmation letter will be sent out.
Example: Shareholder wants to add new banking and establish an AIP:
  • PSS HUB / Retail Brokerage POL Forms /POL- C06 Add/Change Bank Information or PAC Option Form
"IN ORDER TO ESTABLISH AN AUTOMATIC INVESTMENT PLAN YOU ARE REQUIRED TO FILL OUT THE ADD/CHANGE BANK INFORMATION OR PAC OPTION FORM. THAT FORM WILL REQUIRE A NOTARY SEAL BELOW YOUR SIGNATURE. I CAN FAX OR EMAIL THE FORM OR DIRECT YOU TO OUR WEBSITE TO DOWNLOAD THE FORM? MAY I CONFIRM YOUR _____ (FAX/EMAIL ADDRESS)? THE FORM WILL ARRIVE IN 10 MINUTES. IF YOU HAVE ANY QUESTIONS FILLING OUT THE FORM ONCE IT ARRIVES, FEEL FREE TO GIVE US A CALL. WE ARE AVAILABLE FROM 8 AM TO 8 PM MONDAY THROUGH FRIDAY EASTERN TIME. THE REGULAR AND OVERNIGHT MAILING ADDRESSES ARE ON THE TOP OF THE FORM. “ONCE YOUR FORM IS RECEIVED IN GOOD ORDER, BEFORE 4 PM EASTERN TIME ON A BUSINESS DAY, IT WILL BE PROCESSED WITHIN 3 TO 5 BUSINESS DAYS. A CONFIRMATION LETTER WILL BE SENT OUT THE NEXT BUSINESS DAY, ARRIVING TO YOUR ADDRESS WITHIN 7 TO 10 BUSINESS DAYS.

FINANCIAL

  • processed with that night’s market closing price if it arrives in GOOD ORDER before 4pm ET on a business day. A confirmation letter will be sent out.
Example: Minor on a UTMA account has reached the age of majority and wants to redeem account:
  • PSS HUB / Retail Brokerage Distribution Kits / POL- 93 UGMA/UTMA Minor Reached the Age of Majority Redemption / Transfer Form
IN ORDER TO REDEEM FROM YOUR ACCOUNT YOU ARE REQUIRED TO FILL OUT THE UGMA/UTMA MINOR REACHED AGE OF MAJORITY REQUESTS REDEMPTION/TRANSFER FORM. I CAN FAX OR EMAIL THE FORM OR DIRECT YOU TO OUR WEBSITE TO DOWNLOAD THE FORM? MAY I CONFIRM YOUR _____ (FAX/EMAIL ADDRESS)? THE FORM WILL ARRIVE IN 10 MINUTES. A SIGNATURE GUARANTEE STAMP, PREFERABLY A MEDALLION SIGNATURE GUARANTEE STAMP, MUST BE PLACED BESIDES YOUR SIGNATURE. WOULD YOU LIKE ME TO EXPLAIN THE STAMP TO YOU? IF YOU HAVE ANY QUESTIONS FILLING OUT THE FORM ONCE IT ARRIVES, FEEL FREE TO GIVE US A CALL. WE ARE AVAILABLE FROM 8 AM TO 8 PM MONDAY THROUGH FRIDAY EASTERN TIME. THE REGULAR AND OVERNIGHT MAILING ADDRESSES ARE ON THE TOP OF THE FORM. ONCE YOUR FORM IS RECEIVED IN GOOD ORDER, BEFORE 4 PM EASTERN TIME ON A BUSINESS DAY, IT WILL BE PROCESSED WITH THAT NIGHT’S MARKET CLOSING PRICE. A CHECK, ALONG WITH A CONFIRMATION LETTER, WILL BE SENT OUT THE NEXT BUSINESS DAY, ARRIVING TO YOUR ADDRESS WITHIN 7 TO 10 BUSINESS DAYS. WOULD YOU LIKE TO WRITE DOWN THE CONFIRMATION NUMBER FOR THE FORM REQUEST? THE CONFIRMATION NUMBER IS _______.’
