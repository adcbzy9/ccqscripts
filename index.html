SECURITY

"CAN I GET YOUR ACCOUNT NUMBER OR SOCIAL SECURITY NUMBER?"

"CAN YOU BEAR WITH ME FOR A MOMENT WHILE I ACCESS YOUR ACCOUNT?"

"THANK YOU FOR YOUR PATIENCE"
  • CAN I GET YOUR FULL NAME?
    • THANK YOU, MR./MRS. [LASTNAME]
  • ADDRESS?
  • DATE OF BIRTH?
  • CAN YOU ALSO GIVE ME YOUR BEST GUESS OF WHAT YOUR ACCOUNT BALANCE MAY BE IN DOLLARS?
  • Actual Balance < $100,000 - within $10,000
  • Actual Balance > $100,000 – within $100,000
  • The Year in which the account was opened
  • The exact year must be provided
  • Agent Name/Info:
  • Must provide the agents’ full name or Rep ID
  • Recent Transaction:
  • PAC or SWP dollar & frequency i.e. $50/month (exception for PAC & SWP: do not need the date)
  • Must be one of last 3 Transactions initiated either by the Shareholder or the Agent
  • Details needed:
  • Transaction Type
  • Approximate Date (within a week)
  • Approximate Amount (Under $100,0000 - within $10,000 or Over $100,0000 – within $100,000)
  • Fund Position (Holding):
  • Fund symbol, fund name (without class but must be full name and have the fund family, such as Legg Mason and verbal identification of the fund name such as ‘small cap value’) or fund number.
  • MRN or Reference Number for Image
  • 17-digit Reference Number often in the body of letters sent out
  • The shorter 7 digit reference number at the top is the Correspondence ID and cannot be used

For Minors: We will take the same information from the list above but can only act with the Responsible Party/Custodian.


Additionally the TPA also needs to confirm:
  • Their full name
  • The name of the TPA
  • That they are the administrator of the school system listed in the registration

Note: DOB is not applicable for these account types.
  1. Customer name
    • Customer name will have to be individual listed in the acct registration or signed in the legal documents (found in Image), or signed application, or a legal document within 90 days.
  2. Account number or TIN/EIN/SSN
  3. Address
  4. Name of the Entity/Trust
  5. General account balance, or Holding, Transaction last deposit, MRN, Agent name or Rep ID, or year opened
