Redemption


Verify if withdrawal is from principle or earnings.
Master Menu > Inquiry > Ret Plan Participant info

If the Shareholder is under the age of 59 ½: (Non-Qualified Distribution):
“SINCE YOUR ARE UNDER THE AGE OF 59 ½ A PREMATURE DISTRIBUTION PENALTY OF 10% ON ANY EARNINGS MAY APPLY. YOU MAY ALSO BE SUBJECT TO TAXES ON THE EARNINGS PORTION OF THE REDEMPTION.”

If the shareholder is age 59 ½ or older but the account is open less than 5 years:
“SINCE THE ACCOUNT WAS OPENED LESS THAN 5 YEARS AGO, YOU MAY BE SUBJECT TO TAXES ON THE EARNINGS PORTION OF THE REDEMPTION.”

IF THE SHAREHOLDER IS AGE 59 ½ OR OLDER AND THE ACCOUNT IS OPEN 5 YEARS OR MORE (Qualified Dist):
“BASED ON YOUR AGE & HOW LONG YOU’VE HAD THE ACCOUNT, THE REDEMPTION IS NOT SUBJECT TO FEDERAL INCOME TAX.”



IF THE SHAREHOLDER IS UNDER THE AGE OF 59 1/2 (Premature Distribution):
“SINCE YOU ARE UNDER THE AGE OF 59½ A PREMATURE DISTRIBUTION PENALTY OF 10% MAY APPLY. YOU MAY ALSO BE SUBJECT TO TAXES ON THE AMOUNT OF THE REDEMPTION.”

IF THE SHAREHOLDER IS AGE 59 ½ OR OLDER (Normal Distribution):
“YOU MAY BE SUBJECT TO TAXES ON THE AMOUNT OF THE REDEMPTION”

Federal & State Withholding (DO NOT ASK FOR ROTH IRAs) -
“WOULD YOU LIKE US TO WITHHOLD A PERCENTAGE FOR FEDERAL TAXES?”

If the state is a mandatory withholding state and the caller requests federal withholding:
“SINCE YOU HAVE REQUESTED TO WITHHOLD FOR FEDERAL TAXES, (State name) REQUIRES US TO WITHHOLD AT LEAST THE STATE MINIMUM FOR THEM AS WELL.”

To find the state minimum withholding percentage search in PSS HUB by: State Tax Withholding
- Click on State Tax Withholding Job Aid – download PFS State Withholding Matrix & check the State/Flat Tax column

Net vs Gross (IF NOT ALREADY CLARIFIED IN STEP 4) –
“WOULD YOU LIKE THE WITHHOLDING AMOUNT TO BE DEDUCTED FROM THE AMOUNT REQUESTED (GROSS) OR FROM THE REMAINING BALANCE OF THE FUND?” (NET)



If the redemption is $30,000 or more, address or bank account has changed in the last 3 months perform MFA.

Ask how much the shareholder wants to withdraw.

Check the CDSC Calculator – Ledger Menu > Execute > Telephone Redemptions > CDSC Calculator
If a CDSC for Class C:
“AS OF THE PREVIOUS DAYS’S MARKET CLOSE, AND NOT INCLUDING ANY PENDING TRANSACTIONS ON YOUR PART OR OURS, TO REDEEM A NET AMOUNT OF $xxx.xx FROM FUND xxxx, THERE WILL BE AN ESTIMATED CONTINGENT DEFERRED SALES CHARGE OF $xx.xx.”
If no CDSC for Class C or Class A:
“ASSUMING THERE ARE NO PENDING TRANSACTIONS ON YOUR SIDE OR OURS, AS OF THE PREVIOUS BUSINESS DAYS’ MARKET CLOSE, THERE WILL BE NO CONTINGENT DEFERRED SALES CHARGE FOR THE AMOUNT REQUESTED.”

Check if banking is on record if no bank then just offer checking options
Offer at least 2 delivery methods - (Offer fund delivery to both the AOR and the bank (if bank exists), then offer two (2) additional forms of delivery, please see options listed below.)
If banking on file over 30 days:
“WE CAN MAIL A CHECK TO YOUR ADDRESS OF RECORD OR WE CAN SEND THE MONEY TO YOUR (state bank name) ACCOUNT WITH THE LAST 4 DIGITS ENDING IN (last 4 of DDA # ).”
If sent to a bank:
“AUTOMATED CLEARING HOUSE ARRIVES WITHIN 2 TO 3 BUSINESS DAYS AND IS FREE, OR FEDERAL WIRE ARRIVES WITHIN 1 TO 2 BUSINESS DAYS BUT YOUR BANK MAY CHARGE YOU A FEE.”
If no bank on file:
“WE CAN MAIL A CHECK BY FIRST CLASS MAIL WHICH WILL ARRIVE IN 7 TO 10 BUSINESS DAYS OR BY EXPRESS MAIL FOR A $12 FEE WHICH WILL ARRIVE IN 2 TO 3 BUSINESS DAYS.”

Mention any fees & Net vs Gross -
Express mail $12 Partial Red: ASK which fund (if multiple) fee is to be redeemed from, and if it will be deducted from the redemption amount requested (GROSS), or the remaining balance of the fund (NET).
Full Red (or dropping below $100 in last fund position): Termination $30 and Annual Custodian $25
Net vs Gross (IF NOT ALREADY CLARIFIED IN STEP 4) –
“WOULD YOU LIKE THE ___FEE___ TO BE DEDUCTED FROM THE AMOUNT REQUESTED (GROSS) OR FROM THE REMAINING BALANCE OF THE FUND?” (NET)
If the redemption is $30,000 or more, address or bank account has changed in the last 3 months perform MFA.

Process the Trade in CRM and Review the Trade Details - If the redemption amount, per fund, is over $25,000 or a Federal Wire redemption or in the state of CT create a Trade Log in Web Image in Web Image to be QC'd.

“YOU HAVE REQUESTED A REDEMPTION IN THE AMOUNT OF ___ (dollars/shares) FROM (fund/class) ___ WITH THE THE LAST 4 DIGITS OF THE PRIMERICA ACCOUNT NUMBER ENDING IN (last 4) XXXX“

IF BEFORE 4 PM EASTERN TIME:
“THIS REDEMPTION WILL BE PROCESSED AS OF THE MARKET CLOSE TODAY___ (date in CRM)”
IF AFTER 4 PM EASTERN TIME:
“THIS REDEMPTION WILL BE PROCESSED AS OF THE MARKET CLOSE THE NEXT BUSINESS DAY___ (date in CRM).”

CHECK SENT BY FIRST CLASS MAIL:
“A CHECK AND CONFIRMATION LETTER WILL BE SENT TO THE ADDRESS PREVIOUSLY VERIFIED THE NEXT BUSINESS DAY, ARRIVING WITHIN 7 TO 10 BUSINESS DAYS.”
CHECK SENT BY EXPRESS MAIL:
“THE CHECK WILL BE SENT TO THE ADDRESS PREVIOUSLY VERIFIED THE NEXT BUSINESS DAY, ARRIVING WITHIN 2 TO 3 BUSINESS DAYS. A CONFIRMATION LETTER WILL ALSO BE SENT OUT ARRIVING WITHIN 7 TO 10 BUSINESS DAYS.”

MONIES SENT TO THE BANK ACCOUNT BY ACH:
“THE MONEY WILL BE SENT BY AUTOMATED CLEARING HOUSE TO THE BANK PREVIOUSLY VERIFIED THE NEXT BUSINESS DAY, ARRIVING WITHIN 2 TO 3 BUSINESS DAYS. A CONFIRMATION LETTER WILL ALSO BE SENT OUT ARRIVING TO THE ADDRESS OF RECORD WITHIN 7 TO 10 BUSINESS DAYS.”
MONIES SENT TO THE BANK ACCOUNT BY FEDERAL WIRE:
“THE MONEY WILL BE SENT BY FEDERAL WIRE TO THE BANK PREVIOUSLY VERIFIED THE NEXT BUSINESS DAY, ARRIVING WITHIN 1 TO 2 BUSINESS DAYS. A CONFIRMATION LETTER WILL ALSO BE SENT OUT ARRIVING TO THE ADDRESS OF RECORD WITHIN 7 TO 10 BUSINESS DAYS.”

“YOU MAY BE ABLE TO DO A TAX FREE ROLLOVER INTO THIS OR ANOTHER IRA ACCOUNT WITHIN 60 DAYS OF RECEIPT OF YOUR REDEMPTION IF YOU ARE ELIGIBLE. IF IT APPLIES, A TAX FORM WILL BE GENERATED & MAILED TO YOU THE EARLY PART OF NEXT YEAR. CAN YOU VERIFY YOUR PHONE NUMBER FOR ME? DO I HAVE PERMISSION TO SUBMIT YOUR REQUEST?

“WOULD YOU LIKE THE CONFIRMATION NUMBER FOR THE TRADE? THE CONFIRMATION NUMBER IS ___.”
